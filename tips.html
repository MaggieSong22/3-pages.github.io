Tips for Perfect Ratatouille

  • Use fresh, high-quality vegetables.
  • Try adding herbs like thyme, rosemary, or basil.
  • Serve with crusty bread.

Variations of Ratatouille

  • Try adding goat cheese or Parmesan.
  • Layer the vegetables for a more artistic presentation.
  • Use different types of vegetables depending on the season.
